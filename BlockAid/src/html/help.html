Blockchain is the Future!

By integrating blockchain technology into charitable giving, BlockAid ensures that every donation is transparent, traceable, and secure, fostering an environment of trust and accountability.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BlockAid?

BlockAid is a platform that leverages blockchain technology to facilitate secure, transparent, and efficient cryptocurrency donations.

How does BlockAid ensure transparency?

Every donation is recorded on the blockchain, allowing donors to track their contributions and verify the allocation of funds.

Which cryptocurrencies are supported?

Currently, BlockAid supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies, with plans to expand in the future.
